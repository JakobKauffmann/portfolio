Distinguishing AI-Generated Text from Human-Written Text

● Developed a machine learning pipeline to differentiate AI-generated text from human-authored content, achieving over 98% precision, recall, and accuracy. ● Created a balanced dataset of 150,000 entries from GPT-3.5, LLaMA 3.2-3B, and human-written WikiHow articles. ● Implemented CNN, MLP , and AdaBoost models and SBERT , InferSent, and USE embedders with fine-tuning. ● Conducted 27 classification experiments to evaluate different model, embedder, and LLM combinations. ● Converted text into grayscale images using a novel representation approach to enhance feature extraction. ● Utilized Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Tableau, and other tools for development, analysis, and visualization. ● Optimized neural network architectures to mitigate overfitting, reduce training loss, and optimize generalizability. ● Achieved high performance across key metrics with CNN and perfect classification accuracy with MLP . ● Presented research findings using data visualizations to effectively communicate methodologies and insights to an academic audience.
