Project Overview

Developed a Chess AI using the minimax algorithm enhanced with alpha-beta pruning and Zobrist hashing. The model achieved a 98% win rate over 1,000 simulations with an average decision time of 0.5965 seconds. Visualizations showcase key decision-making processes.
